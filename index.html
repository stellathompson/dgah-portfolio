Who am I?

Hello! I'm Stella Thompson, a junior Computer Science major and Music minor at Carleton College. At school, I work as a Prefect and TA for the Computer Science department and work as a Student Manager in the dining halls. For fun, I play on the Club Tennis team, where I'm the Treasurer, practice the Pipe Organ, and host a weekly student radio show.

This past winter, I had the opportunity to extern at Concord USA, where I was a Software Consulting Extern. There, I improved my front-end development skills, learned React, and gained invaluable insight into a career in software consulting. I'm currently seeking a summer internship opportunity in the realm of UX Design or digital archiving.
